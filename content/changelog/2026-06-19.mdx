---
title: Grouped options for select & combobox
description: Organize select and combobox choices under labeled groups, with drag-and-drop.
---

- **Option grouping** — turn on "Group options" for a select or combobox to
  organize its choices under labeled headings. Leave a group name blank to get
  an unlabeled divider between sections.
- **Drag-and-drop** — reorder groups, reorder options within a group, and drag
  options from one group to another. Add or delete groups inline; deleting a
  group with options asks first.
- **Clean generated code** — grouped fields emit a nested
  `[{ label, items }]` constant and render with `SelectGroup`/`SelectLabel` or
  `ComboboxGroup`, for both React Hook Form and TanStack Form. Ungrouped fields
  are unchanged.
